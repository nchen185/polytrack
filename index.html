About PolyTrack

PolyTrack is an exhilarating 3D racing game that challenges players to navigate through increasingly difficult tracks made of colorful geometric shapes. With its minimalist design and intuitive controls, PolyTrack offers a perfect balance of accessibility and challenge.

The game features procedurally generated tracks that ensure a unique experience every time you play. As you progress, the tracks become more complex and the speed increases, testing your reflexes and precision to the limit.

Whether you're looking for a quick gaming session or aiming to top the leaderboards, PolyTrack delivers an addictive racing experience that will keep you coming back for more.

Game Features

  • Procedurally generated 3D tracks
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Minimalist geometric design
  • Intuitive controls for all skill levels
  • Global leaderboards
  • Gamepad support for enhanced control

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • Arrow Keys / WASD Steer your vehicle
  • Space Brake / Reverse
  • Shift Boost (when available)
  • R Restart current track
  • P Pause game

Gamepad Controls

  • Left Stick / D-Pad Steer your vehicle
  • A / X Button Accelerate
  • B / Circle Button Brake / Reverse
  • Y / Triangle Button Boost (when available)
  • Start Button Pause game
