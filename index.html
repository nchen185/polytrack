Game Overview

Polytrack is an exciting 3D racing game that combines stunning low-poly graphics with challenging gameplay. Navigate through intricate tracks, master tight corners, and compete for the best times in this addictive racing experience.

With its unique visual style and precise controls, Polytrack offers a fresh take on the racing genre that will keep you coming back for more. Challenge yourself to improve your times and discover all the tracks!

Key Features

Beautiful Low-Poly Graphics

Immerse yourself in a visually striking world with modern low-poly aesthetics.

Multiple Challenging Tracks

Test your skills on various tracks, each with unique layouts and obstacles.

Precise Controls

Experience responsive and accurate controls that make racing both challenging and rewarding.

How to Play

Use your arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle. Master the art of braking and acceleration to achieve the best times. Learn each track's unique characteristics and find the optimal racing line to set new records.

The game features intuitive controls that are easy to learn but challenging to master. Practice makes perfect - keep trying to improve your lap times and racing techniques!

Racing Tips

  • Brake early for sharp turns
  • Use the racing line to maintain speed
  • Look ahead to anticipate upcoming corners
  • Practice each track to learn its quirks
  • Start slow and gradually increase your speed
